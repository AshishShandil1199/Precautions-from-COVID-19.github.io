Prevention from Corona Virus

Stay Home, Stay Safe

How to prevent

How to prevent virus

Wash your place

When you are going to sit in a place sanitise the place and also sanitise your hand first.Thereafter sit on that place.

Maintain distance

Maintain atleast 1-2 feet distance from other people.

Don't touch face

Do not touch your face. Keep Sanitising your hands after a set of 30 minutes

Wash your hand

Keep washing your hands with soap and start a set of 30 minutes for it regularly.

Use napkin

While sneezing,coughing use handkerchief if not able use napkin and after use that napkin in dustbin.

wear a mask

Always wear a mask and especially when you go outside wear double mask.Don't wear a wet mask or mask with 1 layer . wear good quality mask

Things you should do

Wash your hand

Keep washing your hands with soap and start a set of 30 minutes for it regularly.

Always wear a mask

Always wear a mask and especially when you go outside wear double mask.Don't wear a wet mask or mask with 1 layer . wear good quality mask

Stay home during fever

If you notice any fever symptoms avoid going outside . Stay in home quarintine yourself in a room.Avoid drinking and eating cold things and wear mask.

Things you should Not do

Avoid close contact with animals

Avoid contacting with animals animals are more prone to viruses .If you are having pet use gloves and do bath your pet daily avoid going out.

Avoid close contact with peoples

Maintain proper social distancing , avoid shaking hands and coming in contact with other people.Use sanitiser.

Avoid crowded area

Do not go to crowded areas. Avoid party, functions.
